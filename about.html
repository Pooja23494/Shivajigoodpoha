About us

In 2016, Shanti Food Product began an extraordinary journey, fueled by a mission to provide exceptional Poha without any compromises. Our narrative started with a dedication to supply Poha, an essential in every Indian home, in its most authentic state - devoid of any impurities.

Poha is a highly appreciated snack enjoyed throughout India, and we decided to transform the market with our unique strategy. Without an initial team and fueled only by our resolve, we managed the complete process ourselves - from production to packaging.

Our Guiding Principle is to create products that promote a healthy lifestyle for every family. Due to our increasing popularity and rising demand, we have slowly moved from manual packaging to the implementation of fully automated packaging equipment.
